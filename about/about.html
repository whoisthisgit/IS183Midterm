About SLB

We are the Super Luigi Brothers, often abbreviated as SLB. Founded in Sepetember of 2009 by Kevin James, we have continued to create video games based on our lives ever since. Naturally, certain artistic liberties are always being taken to ensure quality entertainment.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

While the original trilogy's development ended in 2014, more games in the series are still made today, albiet at a much slower pace. Life, although fun, can also be very taxing, and as the years have arrived, so have the responsibilities.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Nevertheless, we, the Super Luigi Brothers team, have poured our hearts and souls into creating these video games for the whole family to enjoy. We hope you will appreciate them as well.
